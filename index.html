Chess

I started my chess journey in October 2022, and since then, the game has become a massive part of my life. Whether I’m studying the Queen's Gambit or competing in high-stakes tournaments, I play with a deep level of focus and determination. Currently, I hold an NW rating of 1228 and a USCF rating of approximately 850. To me, chess is more than just a game of pieces on a board; it’s a mental battlefield that challenges me to think three steps ahead. It has always made me feel completely in the zone—like the rest of the world fades away and the only thing that matters is the next move.
